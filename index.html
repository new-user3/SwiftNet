Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our prepaid WiFi service.

After purchasing a plan, you'll receive an email with the network name (SSID) and password. Simply select our network from your device's WiFi settings, enter the password, and you'll be connected.

When you've used all your high-speed data allocation, your connection will automatically switch to unlimited standard speed (typically 1-2 Mbps) which is sufficient for basic browsing and email.

Yes, you can share your connection with other devices up to the maximum allowed by your plan (3, 8, or 12 devices simultaneously). However, sharing beyond your household may violate our terms of service.

You can check your remaining data by logging into your account on our customer portal, or by sending an SMS with the text "BALANCE" to our service number. You'll also receive notifications when you're nearing your data limit.
